About PEAKFLOW

Our Mission

At PeakFlow, we are dedicated to revolutionizing running performance through cutting-edge technology and data science. Our mission is to provide runners of all levels with sophisticated tools that were once only available to serious athletes.

Kairos 1 represents our first major step in this journey - a precision tool designed to optimize pacing strategies and fuel planning for maximum performance during races.

The Technology

Based on research from this arXiv paper, we analyze your running data to create a personalized model of your unique running profile. By combining this with detailed course information and optional weather data, we can predict optimal pacing strategies that account for terrain, elevation changes, and your personal strengths and weaknesses.

We're constantly refining our models based on performance data and feedback from our user community to ensure the most accurate and useful predictions possible.
